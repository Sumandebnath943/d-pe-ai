Company: Nexora AI
Founded: 2022
CEO: Priya Mehta
Headquarters: Bangalore, India

Product: Nexora Studio — a no-code AI agent builder for enterprises.
Pricing: Free tier (up to 3 agents), Pro ($49/mo, 25 agents), Enterprise (custom pricing).
Target Audience: Non-technical product managers and operations leads in mid-size SaaS companies.

Brand Voice: Confident but approachable. Never condescending. Avoids jargon. Uses short sentences.
Tone: Like a smart friend who happens to be an AI expert.
Forbidden Words: "leverage", "synergy", "cutting-edge", "revolutionary".

Key Differentiator: Unlike competitors (Zapier AI, Make), Nexora Studio lets users define agent "personalities" with natural language — no flowcharts or decision trees.

Customer Pain Points:
- Existing automation tools require technical setup
- AI agents often sound robotic and generic
- Enterprise teams want auditability and version control for their AI agents

Recent Launch: Nexora Studio v2.0 (May 2025) introduced multi-agent collaboration — agents can delegate tasks to each other in a defined hierarchy.

Support Policy: 24/7 chat support for Pro and Enterprise. Email-only for Free tier. Average response time: under 4 minutes.
